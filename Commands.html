Bot Commands

List of all available bot commands below

    ⚒️Moderation Commands⚒️
  • clear (Amount): Clears the specified ammoint of messages
  • ban (user) (reason): Bans the specified user from your server
  • kick (user) (reason): Kicks the specified user from your server
  • mute (user) (time) (reason): Mutes the specified user
  • unmute (user): Unmutes the specified user
  • unbans (user) : Unbans specified banned user from the server
  • announce (message): Announces your message in a stylish embeded style!
  • gstart (duration) (prize): Creates a giveaway!

    • 💰Economy Commands💰
  • beg: Beg people people for coins
  • slot (bet): Place a bet on casino machine if you win you will get double of your bet otherwise you will lose betted coins from your wallet
  • deposit (amount/all): Deposit your wallet money into your bank
  • withdraw (amount/all): Withdraw you bank money into your wallet
  • balance: Check your balance
  • search: Search something and get coins or lose coins!
  • shop : View the shop!
  • daily: Get your daily coins!
  • buy: Buy an item from the shop
  • inventory: See your/someone's inventory

    • 🤣Fun Commands🤣
  • 8ball (querry): Answers to the question asked a in a yes/no answer
  • marry (user): Sends a proposal for marriage to specified user
  • divorce: Divorces without your current life partner
  • kill: Kills the specified user with a funny message
  • spc: Play Stone Paper Scissor against a computer!
  • simprate: Sends a your simprate randomly
  • tictactoe (user): Play tick tac toe with specified user!
  • minecraft (server): Search a minecraft server!

    • 📋Config Commands📋
  • help: Shows all commands that are available
  • changeprefix (prefix): Changes bot's server prefix
  • set_reaction (role) (message_id) (emoji): Sets a reaction role to specified message
